Story:
Welcome to Dragon Repeller. You must defeat the dragon that is preventing people from leaving the town. You are in the town square. Where do you want to go? Use the buttons above.

How to Play:
Use gold to buy weapons in the inn, and heal when needed.
Use the Cave and Forest to train. Fight the dragon when you feel you are ready.

Stats Explanation:
XP: The higher your XP value, the more damage you deal and less damage you take. Also increases your Max HP.

Health: When health hits 0, you can choose to continue or start from the beginning (permadeath).

Gold: Allows you to rest and buy weapons.

Developer Note: This version of the game is much more difficult than the original.
